You are the local Graveyard Keeper and you've noticed many lost souls gathering with no where to go. The only way to help them move on is to collect bones which the graveyard, "residents", reward you with for maintaining the last. Collect enough bones using your tools to buy better tools and eventually help the lost souls get home.
